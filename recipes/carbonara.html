Carbonara

Description

Carbonara Pasta it's a famous Italian recipe, easy to prepare and with a superb taste.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Eggs
  • Bacon
  • Parmesano Cheese
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Boil 1 liter of water. Once the water has boiled, add the contents of the powdered broth envelopes and the spaghetti, cook for the time indicated in the pasta instructions.
  2. Meanwhile, melt the butter in a frying pan, add the chopped bacon and cook until golden. Add the garlic and stir until integrated; turn off the fire.
  3. Remove the noodles from the broth (reserving at least 3 cups of the cooking broth) and add them to the pan with the bacon. Add the beaten eggs, parmesan and stir until combined. Add reserved cooking broth, enough to have a creamy sauce. Serve the pasta immediately, sprinkling fresh pepper.
